Welcome to Emolument Watch

This site is dedicated to tracking and documenting potential financial conflicts of interest under the U.S. Constitutions "emoluments clause." It currently focuses on the U.S. President-elect Donald J. Trump.

Emoluments Clause

A clause in the U.S. Constitution states that: "No Title of Nobility shall be granted by the United States: And no Person holding any Office of Profit or Trust under them, shall, without the Consent of the Congress, accept of any present, Emolument, Office, or Title, of any kind whatever, from any King, Prince, or foreign State."
